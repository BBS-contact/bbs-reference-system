AI Policy

Ethical and transparent use of AI in the BBS ecosystem (development draft).

Status notice: This policy describes design principles and intended safeguards. Operational enforcement, audits, and technical controls are developed and tested in separate internal environments. Updates are tracked in the Change Log.

1. Purpose

The BBS AI Policy defines how AI systems (including LEO) are designed, tested, and governed. The goal is that AI serves humanity, protects fundamental rights, and supports truth-first decision processes.

2. Core Principles

  • Truth First: AI must prioritize truth over manipulation, profit, or ideology.
  • Human-Centered: AI exists to support people, never to dominate or exploit them.
  • Transparency by design: AI outputs should be explainable and traceable where technically feasible and lawful.
  • Justice: AI must not discriminate or reinforce systemic bias.
  • Responsibility: AI remains under human oversight, especially in high-impact decisions.

3. Prohibited Uses

  • No use of AI for coercion, manipulation, or unlawful surveillance.
  • No use for military aggression, disinformation, or social exploitation.
  • No hidden profiling, tracking, or commercial resale of data.

4. Data and Privacy

AI follows the BBS Privacy Policy. Data should be processed minimally, securely, and only for declared purposes. No advertising, no data sale, no hidden tracking.

5. Safety and Oversight

  • Independent reviews and audits are planned as the system reaches operational readiness.
  • Public-facing updates are recorded in the Change Log.
  • Fail-safe mechanisms should prevent unintended harm and allow safe shutdown.
  • Human supervision is required in critical decisions.

6. Liability

AI does not replace human accountability. Decisions influenced by AI must be reviewable by designated human authorities. Responsibility remains with the operators and the organization.

7. Updates

This AI Policy will evolve with new knowledge, technology, and societal needs. All updates will be recorded in the Change Log.
