Legal Statute

Official Statute of BBS: legal status, purpose, governance, and principles.

Article 1 — Name and Legal Status

The name of the organization is "Big Begins Small" (BBS). It is a non-profit organization operating under the laws of its host country, with the right to acquire legal entity status.

Article 2 — Purpose and Objectives

  • To promote ethical innovation and the use of AI for the public good.
  • To protect human dignity, life, and personal development.
  • To build tools and systems that empower individuals and institutions ethically.

Article 3 — Core Principles

BBS shall be governed by the Constitution of BBS, and guided by principles of dignity, transparency, non-violence, transformation, and responsibility.

Article 4 — Legal Capacity

The Organization may enter into contracts, acquire rights and obligations, sue and be sued in its own name. It may open bank accounts, own property, and develop software or systems consistent with its purpose.

Article 5 — Membership

Membership is open to any individual or entity that shares the vision and values of BBS. Membership rights and responsibilities shall be defined in the internal governance policies.

Article 6 — Governance Structure

The governance of BBS shall be collective, without hierarchical leadership. Decisions are made through transparent processes with documented logic, participation, and traceability.

Article 7 — AI and Digital Systems

The organization has the right to develop and deploy AI systems (including LEO) which support its mission, provided such systems comply with relevant ethical and legal frameworks including EU AI Act and GDPR.

Article 8 — Funding and Resources

BBS may receive donations, grants, and other contributions. No part of its net earnings shall inure to the benefit of individuals, except as fair compensation for services rendered.

Article 9 — Amendments

This Statute may be amended by a consensus vote of the core members, provided such amendments are consistent with the BBS Constitution and legal frameworks.

Article 10 — Dissolution

Upon dissolution of the Organization, its assets shall be distributed to a non-profit institution with similar purposes, or used for public benefit in accordance with applicable laws.

Declared by the founders of BBS • Date: July 29, 2025
