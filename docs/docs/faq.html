Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Straight answers about BBS, our principles, and how to get involved.

What is BBS?

BBS (Big Begins Small) is a global movement and platform that puts the human being at the center. Change starts small — one word, one action, one choice.

Is BBS a political or religious organization?

No. BBS is not affiliated with any political party, religion, or ideology. We unite people based on ethics, respect, collaboration, and humanity — regardless of belief.

Can BBS be trusted?

BBS is built around transparency and verifiability. Public documents and updates are published, and integrity mechanisms are developed step-by-step.

Is this achievable?

BBS works step-by-step with experts and communities. This is not utopia — it is a practice of a new approach. Real change starts small.

How can I join?

For now, participation is coordinated through direct contact. We will publish a formal onboarding flow as development progresses.

Contact

How can I help if I have no money?

Your ideas, participation, inspiration, and sharing the message are already meaningful support.

How are donations used?

  • Developing BBS tools (the platform itself)
  • Launching projects in education, health, human rights, ecology, and more

Are personal data stored?

Privacy is a priority. During testing, any demo data may be stored only locally on your device (where applicable). After launch, secure and transparent storage will be implemented.

Didn’t find your question?

Write to us, and we will reply personally or add it here.
