Privacy Policy

Reference publication. This policy describes intended data practices for BBS.

Status notice: This repository is a reference model. Operational services and real data processing rules will be published with explicit release notes.

1. Data We Collect

  • Basic identifiers (e.g. name, email, IP address) when provided
  • Activity events related to system use (when enabled)
  • System metadata when necessary for security and stability
  • Voluntarily submitted content (ideas, forms, projects)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process data only for legitimate purposes:

  • To provide and improve BBS services
  • To ensure security and integrity
  • To allow participation and communication
  • To respond to requests and feedback

3. Legal Basis

Processing is based on consent, legitimate interest, or contractual necessity (depending on the service and context).

4. Storage and Protection

Where operational storage is enabled, BBS aims to apply encryption, access control, and documented retention rules appropriate to the service.

5. Sharing Data

We do not sell personal data. Sharing may occur only when required by law, by mandate, or with explicit consent, depending on the service.

6. User Rights

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (where applicable)
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me

7. Cookies

BBS may use essential cookies for core functionality. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling in the reference model.

8. Contact

Questions: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me

Updates are recorded in the Change Log.
