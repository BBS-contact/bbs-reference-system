Legal Statute

Reference publication (development draft). Governance and legal structure of BBS.

Status notice: This statute text is published as part of the reference model and may be refined during development. Updates are recorded in the Change Log.

Article 1 — Name and Legal Status

The name of the initiative is “Big Begins Small” (BBS). Legal form and registration jurisdiction may be defined as the project progresses and formal entities are established where applicable.

Article 2 — Purpose and Objectives

  • Promote ethical innovation and public-interest technology.
  • Protect human dignity, life, and personal development.
  • Build transparent tools that empower individuals and institutions responsibly.

Article 3 — Core Principles

BBS is guided by truth, dignity, transparency, responsibility, and non-coercion. The Constitution of BBS defines the ethical foundation.

Article 4 — Capacity

As legal entities are established, BBS may enter contracts, acquire rights and obligations, and operate systems consistent with its public mission and applicable law.

Article 5 — Participation

Participation is open to individuals and organizations aligned with BBS principles. Rights and duties may be further defined in governance policies and project rules as development progresses.

Article 6 — Governance

Governance is intended to be transparent and accountable. Decision processes should be documented and reviewable, while protecting personal privacy where required.

Article 7 — Digital Systems

BBS may develop digital systems (including LEO) to support the mission. Deployment must respect ethics, privacy rules, and applicable legal frameworks.

Article 8 — Funding

BBS may receive donations, grants, and contributions. Use of funds should be transparent and aligned with the mission.

Article 9 — Amendments

This statute may be updated as the project evolves, with changes recorded publicly in the Change Log.

Article 10 — Dissolution

If a formal entity is dissolved, remaining assets should be directed to a public-benefit purpose in accordance with applicable law.

Declared by the founders of BBS • Date: July 29, 2025
